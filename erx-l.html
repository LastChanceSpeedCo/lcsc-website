The Problem

Most drivers are guessing

Setup changes based on feel alone are hard to repeat and hard to evaluate. ERX-L gives you the data to confirm what the car actually did — corner speeds, lateral load, braking points — so your adjustments are grounded in what happened, not just what it felt like.

It is not meant to replace the driver. It is meant to help the driver see the run more clearly after the helmet comes off.

Specifications

Hardware & System

Component Details
Main Controller ESP32-S3 based platform for firmware and field validation
GPS Module High-rate receiver — position, speed, course mapping, gate detection
Motion Sensor 6-axis IMU — lateral acceleration, longitudinal acceleration, rotation
Data Storage microSD card, removable, CSV format compatible with Boēthos and spreadsheet tools
Wireless Bluetooth — phone app for course setup and configuration
Power Internal rechargeable Li-ion — no vehicle wiring required
Status Indicators LEDs for GPS lock, Bluetooth, logging state, and system status
Log Format CSV — open format, readable without proprietary software
