The Origin

Built from the driver's seat

LCSC started by building, testing, breaking, and improving real parts on real cars. From FB RX-7 development to the ERX-L data logger, every project starts with a problem found in the garage, paddock, or on course.

Products are developed through firsthand experience, not theory. If it hasn't been tested in competition, it's not ready.
