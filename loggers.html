Data Loggers

The ERX family covers three tiers — entry, mid, and flagship — sharing a common GPS/IMU/SD logging core with increasing capability and expansion.

Entry

ERX-L — Leptos

Battery-powered, GPS + IMU + SD logging on ESP32-S3. Light, slim, and simple to install.

  • GPS + IMU logging at high rate
  • BLE live connection to the Boēthos App
  • SD card storage

ERX-M — Metron

Additional capability and expandability beyond ERX-L. Details in development.

  • Expanded sensor inputs

Flagship — Name TBD

ERX-K

Maximum capability, full expansion, ECU integration. Successor to the original Black Box development prototype. Final product name pending trademark review.

  • CAN bus support
