Why This Kit?

Practical Upgrade Path

Many first-gen RX-7 builds need brake upgrades, but not every car needs an expensive full custom race system. This kit is intended to provide a practical rear disc conversion path for builders who want usable parts and clear documentation.

Made for Builders

The goal is not just to sell brackets. The goal is to provide a complete install path: what parts are needed, how they fit, what to check, and how to maintain the setup.
